A personal brand was a true test to my design competency. This is because it required me to create my own unique brand that represented “me”. It would also require me to have a unified appearance across all self-promotional items. The three self-promotional items that I see as most important are 1) resume, 2) business card , and 3) website . Below are the main design aspects that I took into account throughout all of the items:
- Colours (off-white, dark pink, and grey)
- Typeface ("kolletkif" - sourced from Behance)
- My title ("strategic designer")
