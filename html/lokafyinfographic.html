Challenge
If you have ever travelled before, you probably know how challenging it is to navigate around a foreign city. Alas, maps were invented to help prevent this struggle! So if maps exist, how can city navigation ever possibly be a challenge?

The reason is routed within design. If maps aren't designed in a way that is user friendly, then no one will ever use it.

Unfortunately, poor design is quite common in many city maps. For example, the maps provided for travellers going from Charles de Gaulle Airport to Paris is pretty confusing. You would think that since "How to get to Charles de Gaulle (CDG) Airport to Paris" is one of the most searched question for travellers going to Paris, that the maps would be designed well - but they aren't.

Idea
While working as brand intern for the travelling agency, Lokafy, I was inspired to recreate the CDG Airport to Paris maps in the form of an infographic. It would provide a step-by-step guide on how to get to Paris from the CDG Airport. It also served as a nice promotional piece for Lokafy.

After speaking to some people who have travelled between these two destinations and showing them this infographic, they have told me that this infographic is extremely helpful. So if you plan to travel between these two destinations anytime soon, please feel free to use this infographic! Together we can make travel navigation a better and more easier experience.
