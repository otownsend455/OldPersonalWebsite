Summary
"Nova Fest" was done for a class assignment in order to evaluate the students branding and marketing skills. I choose to promote a fake electronic music festival, "Nova Fest". In order to successfully market this festival, I first had to do market research on the typical electronic music festival-goer, and then effectively apply this research to my designs.
